Flex Grow Example

flex-grow determines how remaining space is distributed. Item 2 grows twice as much as items 1 and 3.

flex-grow: 1
flex-grow: 2
flex-grow: 1

Flex Shrink Example

flex-shrink determines how items shrink when there isn't enough space. Item 3 shrinks three times more than item 1.

flex-shrink: 1
flex-shrink: 2
flex-shrink: 3

Flex Basis Example

flex-basis sets the initial main size of a flex item before remaining space is distributed.

basis: 100px
basis: 200px
basis: 300px

Flex Shorthand Example

flex: grow shrink basis - The shorthand property combines all three properties.

flex: 1 1 100px
flex: 2 1 100px
flex: 1 1 100px
